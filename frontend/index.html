About the Engine

Semantic Vectorization

Unlike traditional keyword-based screening, ResumeRanker uses Sentence Embeddings (specifically the all-MiniLM-L6-v2 model) to understand the semantic context of a resume.

This allows the system to recognize that "React Developer" and "Frontend Engineer proficient in Facebook's UI library" are conceptually similar, even if they share zero keywords.

Supported Formats

  • PDF (Advanced parsing with PyMuPDF)
  • DOCX (Structured parsing)
  • TXT (Raw text)
